Objection Handling
Prospect says…Respond with…
"Profound is the market leader in GEO." Profound has strong positioning in enterprise GEO monitoring — that's fair. They do zero traditional SEO, zero site fixes, and their entry price ($399/mo) only covers 3 AI engines. If you're a Fortune 500 with a dedicated marketing ops team and compliance requirements, it makes sense. For most businesses, it's overkill for the GEO piece alone.
"We need real user interaction data, not synthetic queries." Profound's methodology is genuinely differentiated for that use case. It matters most when you're running large-scale GEO programs where data accuracy drives content investment decisions at scale. At that level of sophistication, Profound is a strong choice. At the SMB and mid-market level, the delta between their data and others' is much smaller than the $399–$thousands pricing delta.
"We need SOC 2 and SSO for compliance." If enterprise compliance requirements are non-negotiable, Profound or Semrush may be the right fit. Worth confirming: is SOC 2 a hard requirement from procurement, or a preference? Many teams in the mid-market don't actually have that as a strict requirement.
